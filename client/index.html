Rules
Welcome to Taboo!
This is a fast-paced team guessing game!

You need at least 4 players to play. Your goal is to reach 250 points as fast as possible.

Each turn, a team will have one player selected to be describer. He will be presented with images which he must describe to his teammates. The game is called Taboo for a reason! The describer must not say the word he is describing. For example, he can't say "cheese" to describe cheese. In the same vein, he can't say "a wall in china" to describe the Great Wall of China.

Guessers will earn full points for their team if the word is spelled correctly and half points if it is spelled incorrectly.

Describing words quickly will gain extra time in the next round! If you have extra time remaining by the end of 5 rounds, a secret bonus round worth double points is unlocked.

Good luck, and remember to have fun!
